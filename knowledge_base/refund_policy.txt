====================================================================
ANAS ELECTRONICS - OFFICIAL REFUND, RETURN, AND EXCHANGE POLICY
====================================================================
Last Updated: 2026

1. STANDARD RETURN WINDOW
Customers are entitled to return any electronic product or gadget purchased from our platform within 30 calendar days from the date of delivery. Returns initiated after the 30-day window will be automatically rejected by our system.

2. ELIGIBILITY & PRODUCT CONDITION
To qualify for a full refund to the original payment method, the returned item must fulfill the following criteria:
- The item must be completely unused and in the exact condition it was received.
- The item must be enclosed in its original product packaging.
- All original accessories, including charging bricks, USB cables, user manuals, and warranty cards, must be included in the return box. 

3. DAMAGED, DEFECTIVE, OR BROKEN ITEMS (THE 48-HOUR RULE)
If a product arrives with physical damage, hardware defects, or a shattered/broken screen, the customer must report the issue to support within 48 hours of delivery. 
- Reports made within 48 hours qualify for a free express replacement or a full immediate refund.
- If physical damage is reported after the initial 48-hour window, it will be treated as user-inflicted damage, rendering it ineligible for a refund or free replacement under this policy.

4. RESTOCKING FEES & SHIPPING COSTS
- Return shipping is completely free for all customers nationwide. Anas Electronics provides a pre-paid shipping label via email once a return request is approved.
- We do not charge any restocking fees for items returned in standard eligible conditions.

5. PROCESSING TIMELINES
Once our warehouse team receives and inspects the returned package, refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days. The funds will appear back in the customer's bank account or credit card depending on their financial institution's processing cycles.
