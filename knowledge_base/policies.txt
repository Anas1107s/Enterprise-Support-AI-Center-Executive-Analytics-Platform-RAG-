--- ANAS ELECTRONICS REFUND POLICY ---
Customers can return any electronic gadget within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. 
To qualify, the item must be in its original packaging with all charging cables included.
If an item arrives with a broken screen or physical damage, it must be reported within 48 hours of delivery to receive a free replacement. Shipping fees for returns are completely free.

--- SHIPPING AND DELIVERY TIMES ---
Standard delivery takes 3 to 5 business days across domestic zones. 
Express delivery takes 1 to 2 business days but costs an extra 150 rupees.
Orders placed after 5:00 PM will be processed on the next business day.

--- REWARDS AND MEMBERSHIP ---
Premium Elite members get a 10% discount on all checkout baskets automatically. Membership costs 500 rupees per year.
