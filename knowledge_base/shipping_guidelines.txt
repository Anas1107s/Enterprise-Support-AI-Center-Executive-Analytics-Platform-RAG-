====================================================================
ANAS ELECTRONICS - LOGISTICS, SHIPPING, AND DELIVERY GUIDELINES
====================================================================
Last Updated: 2026

1. DOMESTIC SHIPPING TIERS & PRICING
Anas Electronics offers two primary shipping tiers for all domestic orders:

- STANDARD DELIVERY: Takes 3 to 5 business days to arrive from the date of shipment. Standard delivery is completely free for all orders, with no minimum purchase requirement.
- EXPRESS DELIVERY: Guaranteed to arrive within 1 to 2 business days. Express delivery incurs an additional flat fee of 150 rupees at checkout.

2. DAILY ORDER PROCESSING CUT-OFF TIME
Our fulfillment warehouses operate Monday through Friday. 
- The daily order processing cut-off time is strictly 5:00 PM. 
- Orders placed before 5:00 PM will be packaged and shipped out on the same business day.
- Orders placed after 5:00 PM, or during the weekend (Saturday and Sunday), will be processed on the next available business day.

3. COURIER PARTNERS & TRACKING
All packages are shipped via our verified third-party courier nodes (BlueDart and Delhivery). As soon as an order leaves our facility, an automated email and SMS containing a unique tracking tracking link will be dispatched to the customer.

4. PREMIUM ELITE MEMBERSHIP BENEFIT
Customers enrolled in our Premium Elite Membership tier receive:
- Automatic free upgrades from Standard Shipping to Express Delivery (1 to 2 business days) on all orders, bypassing the 150-rupee fee.
- An automatic 10% discount applied straight to their final checkout shopping cart basket. 
- Premium Elite Membership costs a flat rate of 500 rupees per year.

5. INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
Currently, Anas Electronics only operates and fulfills shipments within domestic zones. We do not support international shipping, global export processing, or delivery to overseas addresses at this time.
